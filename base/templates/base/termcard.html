Termcard

The termcard is a brief calendar of the Guild's upcoming events and games. This is the termcard for Michaelmas term 2022.

  • 07 October - Fresher's Taster
  • 17 October - Michaelmas Week 2 Game
  • 22 November - Michaelmas Week 7 Game

07 October - Fresher's Taster

The Fresher's Taster Game is a one-evening introduction to the fun to be had in the guild for the freshers, and any other prospective assassins.

17 October - Michaelmas Week 2 Game

The Michaelmas Week 2 Game runs for the week commencing Monday of week 2 (17th of October).

22 November - Michaelmas Week 7 Game

The Michaelmas Week 7 Game runs for the week commencing Monday of week 7 (22nd of November).
